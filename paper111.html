Kaya M., Binli M. K., Ozbay E., Hilmi H., Mishchenko Y. (2018) "A large electroencephalographic motor imagery dataset for electroencephalographic brain computer interfaces.", Scientific Data, 5, 180211

In this work, we release a large set of EEG BCI data collected during the development of a slow cortical potentials-based EEG BCI. The dataset contains 60?h of EEG recordings, 13 participants, 75 recording sessions, 201 individual EEG BCI interaction session-segments, and over 60?000 examples of motor imageries in 4 interaction paradigms. The current dataset presents one of the largest EEG BCI datasets publically available to date. Full text
