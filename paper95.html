Yuriy Mishchenko Papers:
Mishchenko Y., Ji C.-R. (2017) "Dark matter phenomenology of high speed galaxy cluster collisions.", European Physical Journal, 77, 505; arXiv:1511.00597

The paper provides a numerical study of various possible conditions of colliding galaxy clusters and searches for signatures that could be left in such collisions by weak self-interactions of dark matter. Certain such signatures are found and related to some recent observations in galaxy cluster collisions' astrophysics. Full text
