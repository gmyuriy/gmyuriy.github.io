Graduate and undergraduate scholarship available at the Non-Invasive Brain-Machine Interfaces Laboratory


My Non-Invasive Brain-Machine Interfaces Laboratory works on developing software and hardware methods for brain-machine/brain-computer interfaces and neural prosthetics. I am looking for part-time or full-time students to contribute to these activities.

Briefly, Brain-Machine Interfaces are novel devices designed to interface directly with the brain and provide communication and control capabilities relying directly on subject's brain or mental processes. This recent field of basic and applied research had recently scored several ground-breaking achievements, such as controlling a computer or a robot arm using only mental activity.

For this project, we are looking for part-time or full-time graduate and undergraduate students with interest in electronics, advanced mathematics, computer science/programming, advanced statistics, and computer modeling. Financial support is available for such students via TUBITAK ARDEB 1001 grant and other sources.

With inquiries please contact myself directly via yuriy.mishchenko@gmail.com.




Non-Invaziv Beyin-Makine Arayuzu Laboratuvarinda lisans ve yuksek lisans/doktora ogrencisi burslari


Non-Invaziv Beyin-Makine Arayuzu laboratuvarimiz, beyin-makine/beyin-bilgisayar diye adlandirilan arayuzleri ve noral protezler icin donanim ve yazilim yontemlerinin gelistirilmesi uzerine calismaktadir. Laboratuvarimizin projelerine katkida bulunacak part-time veya full-time ogrenciler aranmaktadir.

Kisaca Beyin-Makine Arayuzleri, beyin aktivitesi ve zihinsel sureclerine direkt olarak dayali dis-cihazlarla iletisim ve kontrol olanaklari saglayan yeni iletisim cihazlaridir. Cok hizli gelisen ve hem temel hem de uygulamali bir bilim alani olan bu calismalarda, zihinsel aktivite ile bir bilgisayari veya robot kolunu kontrol edebilmek gibi cok onemli basarilar son zamanlar saglanmistir.

Bu tur projelerimize katkida bulunacak, elektronik, ileri matematik, bilgisayar bilimi, bilgisayar programlama, istatistik ve bilgisayar modelleme alanlarinda ilgi ve becerisi olan yari veya tam zamanli lisans veya yuksek lisans ogrencileri aranmaktadir. Basvurular arasindan secilecek ogrenciye, TUBITAK ARDEB 1001 proje tarafindan desteklenen burs saglanabilecektir.

Ilgilenen ogrencilerin yuriy.mishchenko@gmail.com'a direkt olarak basvurmasi gerekmektedir.
